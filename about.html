About Diane White

Bold, expressive fine art shaped by atmosphere, colour, and emotion. Diane White paints the feeling behind a subject — translating nature, mood, and memory into vibrant works that are energetic, recognisable, and deeply personal.

Biography

Diane was born and educated in Manchester, England, where she studied art under Harold Riley, one of England’s top artists. She arrived in South Africa in 1971 and worked for many years as an architectural draughtswoman in Somerset West before taking up painting again in the mid-1990s.

Her work hangs in many private collections around the world. She is represented in various galleries in South Africa and has her own gallery in Stilbaai.

Approach & Subject Matter

Diane loves to experiment with different techniques, styles and media so that her work is constantly evolving and changing. Her dynamic subject matter includes landscapes, cityscapes, wildlife, portraits, floral studies and flow art.

Even though her work continues to evolve, she has an individual and recognisable style and is always open to new ideas and techniques.

Awards & Exhibitions

2020: Landscape / Dreamscape / Seascape — Solo Exhibition at Bright Street Gallery, Somerset West.

2018: Top Gem in the Western Cape Artists’ Society “Little Gems” exhibition; highly commended (Watercolour) and commended (Acrylic) in the WCAS Annual Merit Exhibition.

2017: Highly commended (mixed media) in the South African Society of Artists’ Annual Exhibition.
