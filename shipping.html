Shipping

Once an artwork enquiry is confirmed, Diane White Art will contact you directly to arrange collection or delivery and provide a shipping quote where needed.

Collection and local delivery

  • Collection can be arranged from the gallery in Stilbaai West by appointment.
  • Local delivery may be possible depending on location and artwork size.

Courier delivery

  • Courier delivery can be arranged within South Africa.
  • Shipping costs depend on the size, weight, framing, and destination of the artwork.
  • All artworks will be packaged carefully for safe transport.

International shipping

  • International delivery may be available on request.
  • Customs duties, import taxes, or other charges in the destination country are the responsibility of the buyer.

Delivery times

Delivery times vary depending on destination, courier availability, and whether the artwork is framed or requires special packing. An estimated delivery timeframe will be shared once arrangements are confirmed.

Please note

If you have specific shipping requirements, need insured delivery, or would like a quote before purchase, please use the contact page and Diane will advise you directly.
